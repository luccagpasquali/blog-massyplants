Do you offer maintenance plans for ongoing care of indoor plants?

Yes, we offer maintenance plans tailored to your specific needs, including regular watering, pruning, cleaning, and fertilizing to ensure your indoor plants remain healthy and vibrant.

Can you provide recommendations for plant placement and arrangement to complement my space?

Absolutely! Our team can provide expert advice on plant placement and arrangement to complement your space, taking into account factors such as lighting, space constraints, and aesthetic preferences.

What steps do you take to ensure the health and longevity of the plants you install?

We take great care to select healthy plants and ensure they are properly acclimated to their new environment. We also provide thorough care instructions to our clients and are always available to address any concerns or questions they may have.

Are your indoor landscaping services suitable for both residential and commercial spaces?

Yes, our indoor landscaping services are suitable for both residential and commercial spaces, including offices, restaurants, retail stores, and more.
